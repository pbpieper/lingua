# Lingua — Executive Summary

**An AI-powered language learning platform built on research, not shortcuts.**

---

The global language learning market is projected to reach $60 billion by 2028, driven by immigration, globalization, and workforce development. Yet the dominant platforms share a common weakness: they treat language as a game of isolated drills rather than what decades of second language acquisition (SLA) research tells us it is — a skill built through meaningful exposure to comprehensible input.

Lingua was built to close that gap.

## What Lingua Is

Lingua is a comprehensive language learning platform with 21 integrated tools spanning every dimension of language acquisition: vocabulary building, reading comprehension, writing practice, speaking and listening exercises, grammar instruction, and long-term retention through state-of-the-art spaced repetition. Powered by local AI models via Ollama, Lingua generates context-rich exercises, graded reading material, and real-time writing feedback — all without sending a single byte of learner data to the cloud. A dedicated Teacher Portal enables classroom deployment with class management, vocabulary assignments with criteria and deadlines, and granular student progress tracking. The platform is production-ready: built on Vite, React 19, and TypeScript with a clean 230KB bundle and full code splitting.

## Key Differentiators

- **Reading-first methodology** grounded in Stephen Krashen's Comprehensible Input hypothesis — the most empirically supported framework in SLA research. Lingua's Pre-Learn Pipeline and AI-generated graded stories move learners from vocabulary recognition to authentic reading faster than drill-based competitors.

- **FSRS-4.5 spaced repetition** — the most advanced open scheduling algorithm available, delivering approximately 30% fewer reviews than the SM-2 algorithm used by Anki and most competitors, with equal or better long-term retention.

- **Privacy-first AI architecture** — all language generation, writing correction, and content creation runs locally on-device through Ollama. Zero cloud dependency means zero data exposure, a decisive advantage for government, military, and K-12 deployments where data sovereignty is non-negotiable.

- **Teacher and classroom tools** — class creation with join codes, vocabulary assignments with custom criteria and deadlines, per-student progress dashboards, and completion tracking. No major competitor offers integrated instructor tooling at this level.

- **21 integrated tools in a single platform** — vocabulary upload, FSRS flashcards with tag filtering, match games, fill-in-the-blank, multiple choice quizzes, speaking practice, interactive reading with hover-translate, listening dictation, AI writing correction, sentence cloze exercises, AI-generated stories, grammar lessons, a vocabulary universe visualization, achievements and XP, a progress dashboard, community features, and a Chrome Extension for capturing vocabulary from any webpage.

- **Accessibility and internationalization** — full RTL support for Arabic, Hebrew, Farsi, and Urdu; ARIA roles and keyboard navigation throughout; CEFR-level tagging on vocabulary for level-aware exercises.

## Market Opportunity

Lingua is designed to serve four distinct market segments:

- **B2C — Self-directed learners.** Travelers, expatriates, hobbyists, and professionals who want a research-backed alternative to gamified apps that plateau after beginner levels.

- **B2B Education — K-12, universities, and language schools.** The Teacher Portal transforms Lingua from a personal study tool into a classroom platform. Instructors assign targeted vocabulary, set deadlines, and monitor student progress — capabilities that typically require a separate LMS.

- **B2B Enterprise — Corporate language training.** Multinational companies investing in employee language skills gain a privacy-compliant, self-hosted solution with measurable outcomes via FSRS retention metrics.

- **Government — Immigration and military programs.** WIOA-funded immigration and workforce integration programs need scalable, accessible language tools. The Defense Language Institute (DLIFLC) and DLPT preparation demand rigorous, skills-based training across all modalities — reading, listening, speaking, and writing — which Lingua delivers in a single platform with no cloud data exposure.
